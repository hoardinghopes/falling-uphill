Principles of Mind and Body Coordination

There are 4 Principles of Coordination of Mind and Body . They create a deliberate state of being which I would categorise as one of well-being, aliveness, and wakefulness. It feels like the sense of aliveness one has when on a good holiday – well rested, peaceful, at ease – but these are not just pleasant, it ensures that the body is effective and efficient, well-balanced and resilient.

We use the following principles as gateways into this coordinated state, and aspire to maintain this state throughout our aikido exercises, and then into the rest of our lives.
